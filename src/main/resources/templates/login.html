Welcome to Food@Home

We all know that cooking food at home is almost always cheaper and healthier. However, for many of us, having the right ingredients available for a good meal is an elusive goal.

Food@Home helps you cook at home more often by helping you define a basic list of groceries you want to keep stocked at home.

Once you have the list set up, you can easily walk through the kitchen with your phone and update the current status of all your basic items. All you need to do is update the status with a single click and then submit your changes.

Planning a meal? Select "In Stock Items" to see only items you currently have in your kitchen.

Planning at trip to the grocery store? Select "Shopping List" to see only items that need replenishing.

Underneath the list you'll find buttons to add new items and categories. Or, click on an item name to update its details.

Food@Home is currently in beta. If you encounter any issues or have any questions you can drop us an email.
